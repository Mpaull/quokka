Habit Overview

Over the past few weeks, you’ve been learning a lot about what you can do to improve your well-being. This week is an opportunity for you to to do some research on your own! The focus of this week is on how foods can affect how we feel, both physically and emotionally. Besides helping you stay in shape, healthy foods can help you increase your energy, boost your immune system, minimize inflammation, promote muscle building, and more.

Week 4 Challenge

Take the first day of this week to check out the Harvard School of Public Health’s Healthy Eating Plate Guide on how to make better food decisions. Pick what will be an improvement for your health and then stick to it for every day this week.
